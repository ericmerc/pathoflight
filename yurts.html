The Yurts at Pacific Trails

What is a Yurt?
Our luxury yurtd are permanent structures four feet off the ground. Each yurt is fully enclosed with canvas walls, a wooden floor and a roof dome that can be opened.
How are Yurts Finished
Each yurt is fuenished witha queen-size bed with down quilt and gas-fired stove. your luxury camping experience includes electricity and a sink with hot and cold running water. shower and restroom facilities are located in the lodge.
What should i bring?
Most guests pack comfortable wakking shoes and plan to dress for changing weather with light layers of clothing. it's also helpful to bring a flasghlight and sense of adventure!
